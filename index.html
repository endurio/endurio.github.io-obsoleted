Double Tokens Stablecoin

By contracting and expanding the circulating supply, the NDR token is able to absorb all the market volatility of STB token, keeping its price stabilized around a target value chosen by the network. Selective Staking Proof of Work

S2PoW is a hybrid consensus, with PoW and PoS tightly coupled together to power a fully decentralized, highly available and energy efficient public blockchain. A Staking Service Network double the security of the chain by allowing participants to stake their holding to serve and protect the system.

Economic-driven Sharding Protocol

Yggdrasil sharding protocol dynamically split the workload to more shards as the chain is reaching saturation. Shards are split by transaction values and protected with proportional mining and staking power. This economic-driven property allows Endurio to sidestep many challenges other pure-technical sharding protocols have to face.

Instant Confirmation

Input Locking Service provides instant confirmation with 100% warranty of no double-spend for all transactions. This allows Endurio to have a pleasing user experience while keeping the block time long enough to preserve the security and consistency of the chain.

Effortless On-chain Governance

Voting by Activity allows all participants (miners, staking service nodes and users) to vote for the future of the project by simply doing what they do every day - mining new block, serving SSN and performing everyday transactions.

Minimal Transaction Fee

Blockchain transaction fee is always zero from the beginning, it is the limited scalability that forces the users to compete for the service with a higher fee. Endurio, with sharding, is able to serve any amount of transactions requested by the network without raising the fee.

Disruptive Combination of Technology

Built on a tightly-coupled hybrid consensus, S2PoW preserves the competitive nature of the time-tested PoW, with much less energy consumption and allows the stakeholders to intrinsically participate in securing the consensus. With our novel Staking Service Network and Economic-driven Sharding Protocol, many advanced services and features can be delivered with unlimited scalability for the payment network.

Technical

1How does Yggdrasil compare to Ethereum Sharding?

Our sharding protocol is economic-driven. It sidesteps many challenges other pure-technical sharding protocols (like Ethereum) have to face, including:

  • • Scalability and decentralization of super-full and top-level nodes (where all state from all shards is verified).
  • • High frequency of cross-shard communication and transactions.

Yggdrasil sharding protocol has no super-full node, each node always works on only one shard at a time, and most of the transactions occur locally in one shard. These features make itâ€™s extremely efficient scaling solution, just like the sharding architect of the traditional database.
